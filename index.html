Ainavilli Vinayaka Temple

Sacred Siddhi Vinayaka Shrine of Andhra Pradesh

About the Temple

Ainavilli Vinayaka Temple is one of the most ancient and revered temples dedicated to Lord Ganesha. The presiding deity is worshipped here as Siddhi Vinayaka.

Devotees strongly believe that prayers offered at this sacred temple remove obstacles and bring success, peace, and prosperity.

Spiritual Significance

The temple holds immense spiritual importance. Many devotees visit Ainavilli Vinayaka Temple before starting new ventures, marriages, or important life events to seek divine blessings.
